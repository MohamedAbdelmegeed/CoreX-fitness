THE COREX STORY

Founded in the heart of Cairo, CoreX Fitness was born from a vision to bridge the gap between "standard workouts" and "elite performance."

Our founder, Ahmed Hassan, noticed that many local gyms lacked the specialized equipment and the high-energy atmosphere required for true physical transformation. He set out to create a sanctuary for those who refuse to settle for average.

Today, CoreX stands as a testament to discipline. We don't believe in shortcuts or "magic" fixes. We believe in the raw power of iron, the science of recovery, and the unbreakable spirit of our community.

"Your body is a project that never ends. At CoreX, we provide the tools, but you provide the soul."

— Ahmed Hassan, Founder
