Our weight room isn't just a collection of machines. We feature competition-grade powerlifting racks, Olympic platforms, and a massive selection of free weights up to 60kg. Whether you are a beginner or a pro, we have the iron you need.

MMA & COMBAT SQUARE

Unleash your inner warrior in our dedicated combat zone. Featuring a professional-grade cage area and heavy bags for Kickboxing, Muay Thai, and Jiu-Jitsu. Classes are led by active competitors from the Cairo MMA scene.

Training is only half the battle. Our recovery wing features high-heat saunas, eucalyptus steam rooms, and sports massage therapy to ensure your muscles recover faster and you stay injury-free.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! 6 and 12-month plans allow for a 15-30 day "Freeze" period if you are traveling or injured.

While the gym floor is open, personal trainers are available by appointment from 6 AM to 11 PM.
